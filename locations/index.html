FAQ

Common Questions

What areas do you serve? expand_more

We serve 52+ cities across three counties: New Castle County in Delaware, and Delaware County and Chester County in Pennsylvania. From Wilmington to West Chester, Upper Darby to Phoenixvilleif you're within our service area, we can help.

How soon can you get to my location? expand_more

Response times vary by location and current demand. When you call, we dispatch the nearest available technician and give you an honest arrival estimate upfront. Our local presence throughout Delaware and PA means shorter drive times for most areas.

Do you charge extra for locations farther from your shop? expand_more

We provide transparent pricing before starting any work. While our base location is in Delaware, we have mobile units operating throughout our service area. Your quoted price is your final priceno hidden travel surcharges.

Are you certified to work in both Delaware and Pennsylvania? expand_more

Yes. We hold active locksmith licenses in both states and carry comprehensive liability insurance. Every technician on our team passes a background check and receives ongoing training.

Can you service commercial properties in my area? expand_more

Absolutely. We serve businesses throughout all three counties with commercial locksmith services including master key systems, access control, high-security locks, and emergency lockout response.

What if my city isn't listed on your website? expand_more

If you're within our general service area (New Castle County DE, Delaware County PA, or Chester County PA) but don't see your specific town listed, give us a call. We likely serve your area and can confirm availability.
