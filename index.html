FAQs

Common Questions

help question_mark

Are you available during operating hours?

add

Yes! We offer prompt emergency locksmith services for lockouts and urgent security needs. Our mobile service operates Sunday through Thursday 7AM-10PM and Friday 7AM-4:30PM. We are closed on Saturdays. Our storefront is open Sunday through Friday 9AM-4PM.

Is there an extra charge for after-hours or weekend service?

add

We keep our after-hours response transparent. While some companies charge extreme premiums at night, our after-hours diagnostic quotes remain consistent. We'll always provide a transparent diagnostic quote upfront before we startâ€”no surprises when you're already stressed.

My key broke off inside the lock. Can you help?

add

Absolutely. Broken key extraction is one of our specialties. We use specialized tools to remove the broken piece without damaging your lock. In most cases, we can also cut you a new key on-site so you're not stranded.

There's a child or pet locked in my car. What do I do?

add

If a child or pet is locked in a vehicle, call us immediately. We treat these situations as our absolute top priority. We will drop everything to dispatch the nearest available technician to your location instantly. While you wait, keep the child or pet as calm as possible and stay in direct communication with our dispatch team.

Do I need to prove I own the property during a lockout?

add

Yes, for your protection and ours. We require valid ID matching the address, registration for vehicles, or other proof of residency. This protects you from unauthorized entry. If you're a renter, a lease or utility bill works too.

What should I do while waiting for the locksmith?

add

Prioritize your safety by staying in a well-lit area or nearby open business. Please avoid attempting forced entry or DIY bypass techniques, as these often lead to structural hardware damage that can complicate the resolution and lead to unnecessary hardware replacement. Have proof of property ownership ready to present to our technician upon arrival. We will maintain communication as our mobile lab transit to your location.
