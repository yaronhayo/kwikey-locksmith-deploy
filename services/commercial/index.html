The Issue

Common Questions

Commercial Locksmith FAQ

help question_mark

What's the difference between key cards and fob access?

add

Both use RFID technology. Key cards are credit-card sized and often more practical. Fobs are keychain-sized and more convenient. Both can be instantly deactivated when an employee leaves. We install and program both types.

An employee left with keys. What should I do?

add

Rekey immediately. We can rekey your entire business professionally and affordablyoften in a single visit. With access control systems, you can delete their credential in seconds. Either way, don't wait. Call us today for peace of mind.

How does a master key system work?

add

A master key system lets different keys open different doors while one master key opens them all. Perfect for property managers, office buildings, and retail stores. Each employee gets access only to their areas while owners maintain full access.

What is panic bar or crash bar hardware?

add

Panic hardware is the push-bar exit device required by fire code on many commercial doors. It allows immediate emergency exit while remaining secure from the outside. We install, repair, and maintain all types of panic hardware.

What are the advantages of a commercial master key system?

add

A master key system allows business owners to access every door with a single key while restricting specific employees to their designated areas. This enhances internal security and simplifies access management for complexes and office buildings.

Can you open my commercial safe if I forgot the combination?

add

Yes, we are highly trained safe technicians. We can open most commercial safes without damage using manipulation techniques or specialized tools. We can also change the combination after and provide documentation for your records.
