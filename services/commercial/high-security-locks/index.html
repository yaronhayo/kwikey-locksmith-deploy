Frequently Asked

High-Security Locks FAQs

An employee left with keys. What should I do?

expand_more

Rekey immediately. We can rekey your entire business professionally and affordably—often in a single visit. With access control systems, you can delete their credential in seconds. Either way, don't wait. Call us today for peace of mind.

Do you offer commercial security assessments?

expand_more

Yes, we provide comprehensive security audits for businesses. We'll evaluate all entry points, locks, and access control systems, then provide recommendations based on your risk level and budget. Many clients find vulnerabilities they never knew existed.

Can you integrate access control with my existing doors?

expand_more

Usually yes. We can retrofit most commercial doors with card readers, keypads, or fob systems. We'll assess your current hardware and recommend the most effective solution that meets your security needs.

Are your locksmiths background-verified?

expand_more

Yes, every technician on our team undergoes rigorous multi-stage background verification and carries comprehensive professional insurance. We prioritize your security by ensuring our staff are verified experts in residential, commercial, and automotive lock systems.

Are your locksmiths background checked?

expand_more

Yes, every technician undergoes a thorough background check before joining our team. We also carry full insurance. You're trusting us with access to your home or business—we take that responsibility seriously.

What's the difference between key cards and fob access?

expand_more

Both use RFID technology. Key cards are credit-card sized and often more practical. Fobs are keychain-sized and more convenient. Both can be instantly deactivated when an employee leaves. We install and program both types.

High-Security Locks in Delaware & Pennsylvania

When standard locks aren't enough, KWIK LOCKSMITH provides high-security lock solutions throughout Delaware and Greater Philadelphia. We're authorized dealers for Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, and other premium brands that provide verified protection against picking, bumping, drilling, and unauthorized key copying.

Why Standard Locks Fail

YouTube has thousands of videos showing how to pick standard deadbolts in seconds. Bump keys open most residential locks without leaving a trace. A cheap cordless drill with a cheap bit goes through standard cylinders in under a minute. High-security locks are specifically engineered to defeat these common attack methods. If you're protecting something valuable, standard locks are theater—not security.

Key Control Is the Overlooked Essential

Maybe your locks are fine, but who has copies of your keys? Previous employees, old roommates, contractors, the dog walker from three years ago? With standard locks, anyone with a key can make unlimited copies at any hardware store. High-security restricted keyways require your authorization card for every duplicate. You finally know exactly who has access.
