Expertly Resolved FAQ: High-Security Locks

What type of lock is recommended for a storefront glass door? expand_more

We typically recommend Adams Rite or similar storefront locks designed specifically for aluminum-frame glass doors. They're secure, durable, and designed for high-traffic commercial use. We carry parts for most major brands.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work? expand_more

We provide a 90-day service commitment on all workmanship and hardware installations. If you encounter any operational issues during this period, we will return to your location for a diagnostic assessment and resolution as part of our service pledge. Your security and peace of mind are our primary operational priorities.

What information do you need when I call? expand_more

For the most efficient service, have ready: your location (address or cross streets), type of lockout (home, car, business), vehicle year/make/model if automotive, and any details about your lock type if you know them. This helps us dispatch the right tech with the right tools.

How soon can you get to me? expand_more

Arrival times are subject to location, traffic conditions, and current dispatch volume. We prioritize emergency lockout requests and maintain a network of mobile technicians across the region to ensure a professional response. We provide an honest, professional arrival estimate during your initial consultation and maintain active communication as our technicians depart.

Are your locksmiths background checked? expand_more

Yes, every technician undergoes a thorough background check before joining our team. We also carry full insurance. You're trusting us with access to your home or businesswe take that responsibility seriously.

Should my safe be bolted to the floor? expand_more

Absolutely, for any safe under 750 lbs. Thieves often steal smaller safes to crack later at their leisure. Bolting eliminates this risk. We include professional anchoring in our safe installation service.

Why Standard Locks Fail

YouTube has thousands of videos showing how to pick standard deadbolts in seconds. Bump keys open most residential locks without leaving a trace. A cheap cordless drill with a cheap bit goes through standard cylinders in under a minute. High-security locks are specifically engineered to defeat these common attack methods. If you're protecting something valuable, standard locks are theaternot security.

Key Control Is the Overlooked Essential

Maybe your locks are fine, but who has copies of your keys? Previous employees, old roommates, contractors, the dog walker from three years ago? With standard locks, anyone with a key can make unlimited copies at any hardware store. High-security restricted keyways require your authorization card for every duplicate. You finally know exactly who has access.
